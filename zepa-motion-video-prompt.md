## PROMPT

Create a 35-second cinematic product launch video titled **"Introducing Zepa"** — a premium React/Next.js UI component library. Format 16:9, 1920×1080, 30fps. Tone: dark, premium, confident, modern-tech.

**BRAND & COLOR — strict:**
- Background: pure black (#000000).
- Primary accent: deep matte navy blue **#072b5b** (also use #0a3a7a as a lighter step). This is an ink/liquid blue, NOT neon.
- **NO glow, NO bloom, NO neon, NO light rays, NO lens flare.** Keep the blue flat and matte, like deep-ocean ink diffusing through black. Soft, not electric.
- Text: off-white #f5f2f0 (primary), muted gray #9a9a9a (secondary).
- Optional: a very faint 1px grid (#141414) far in the background, barely visible.
- Typography: clean modern geometric sans (Inter / Geist style), tight tracking, large bold weights.
- Motion: smooth, weighty, slow-ish easing. Elegant, not hyperactive. Subtle depth/parallax and gentle 3D tilt on panels.

**AUDIO:** minimal ambient electronic bed, deep low sub-bass hit on the logo reveal, soft airy whoosh on transitions, building subtly toward the outro. No voiceover.

**SCENE-BY-SCENE:**

**0:00–0:04 — Logo intro.** Black screen. Deep navy-blue ink (#072b5b) slowly swells and diffuses across the frame like liquid smoke through black (matte, no glow). The wordmark **"zepa"** resolves from a heavy motion-blur — letters drift in blurred, then snap into sharp focus. Low sub-bass hit as it locks.

**0:04–0:09 — Hook.** Ink settles. Bold kinetic type animates in, word by word: **"Build faster."** (white) then **"Build smarter."** (gray). Centered, large. Subtle blue ink drifting behind.

**0:09–0:13 — Positioning.** Type crossfades to a smaller line: **"Premium components, motion systems, and immersive UI patterns."** Held briefly, calm.

**0:13–0:25 — Component showcase (the core).** Use the **5 component preview videos I provide** — display each inside a floating card / glass browser-frame drifting in 3D space over the black + matte-blue background. Each card plays its preview video. Move through all 5 as confident quick cuts or a slow carousel rotation (cards curve toward camera, gentle 3D tilt + parallax). Cards have matte edges with a thin #072b5b border and rounded corners — **no glow**. Small kicker labels can fade beside them (e.g. **"WebGL" · "3D" · "GSAP" · "Scroll-driven"**). Each preview card holds ~2–2.5s.

**0:25–0:29 — Product reveal.** Panels converge into one hero screen: the Zepa landing page ("Build faster. Build smarter.") tilts into view as a clean browser mockup, then settles flat and centered.

**0:29–0:33 — Proof.** Quick confident stats animate up over black: **"30+ components"** then **"Trusted by 200+ developers."** Minimal, big numbers in white, labels in gray.

**0:33–0:37 — Outro CTA.** Everything clears to black. Blue ink gently blooms once more (matte). Centered: the **"zepa"** wordmark, with **"Build faster. Build smarter."** beneath, then **"zepa.design"** and a pill button reading **"Browse Components."** Hold 1.5s, fade to black.

Keep the whole piece cohesive: black + matte deep-blue #072b5b only, elegant weighty motion, zero glow.
